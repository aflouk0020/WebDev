WELCOME TO BALLINASLOE GOLF CLUB

Ballinasloe Golf Club, founded in 1894, is one of Connacht's oldest golf clubs. It is a mature and testing parkland course, with excellent greens and is playable all year round.

Club Lotto - Over €25,000 Won to Date - Jackpot €5.000 Have you joined the Club Lotto? (Entry Forms in Office) Draw every month.

The Ballinasloe Academy & Driving Range is a 10 minute drive from Ballinasloe Golf Club on the old Athlone road (R446). The range is floodlight and offers both sheltered practice bays and a grass practice area. Range balls are available for rent. For full details on prices and opening hours

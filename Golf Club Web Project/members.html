New Members Welcome

Ballinasloe Golf Club is welcoming new members throughout the year for all age groups. We are proud to present a mature 18 golf course which offers a strategic challenge as you work your way through the dogleg holes on the outward nine and then finish with longer and straighter holes on the inward nine. Overall the 18 holes offer a quality to test to golfers of all abilities.

The course is equipped with buggy paths, practice area, practice nets, and floodlight chipping & putting greens, which are rare to find in the region. We also provide drinking water taps on course at the 3rd, 8th, and 15th holes, and toilet facilities after the 9th hole.

The club house facilities include separate men's and women's locker rooms & showers, and a bar & restaurant which is a great place to socialise with and get to know other members in the club.

Golf lessons are available at the golf club or alternatively at Ballinasloe Academy & Driving Range. Please see the notice boards in each venue for contact details of professional golf instructors. Lessons are a great way to get to grips with the basics of the golf swing for new golfers or to refine an experienced swing for more consistent shots.

We have a wide variety of membership plans that are available on our website or by calling the office on 0909642126. Membership is open to men, women, boys, and girls.

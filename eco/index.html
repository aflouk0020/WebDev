Welcome to Eco Ireland

Eco Ireland brings balance back into the Irish Environment

Our site is devoted to putting Ireland at the forefront of the green revolution - our aims are to encourage everyone in Ireland to minimise global warming and pollution.

These are just some of the ways in which you can help:

  1. Walk or cycle to school or work
  2. Use public transport
  3. If you have to drive a car, use an energy efficient one
  4. Insulate your house
  5. Switch off electric appliances at source
  6. Use long life light bulbs
  7. Use renewable clean fuels
  8. Buy local produce in season
  9. Buy loose/unpackaged produce
  10. Re-use glass jars, plastic containers, etc.
  11. Recycle only what you cannot re-use
  12. Compost kitchen and garden waste
  13. Create a wild area in a corner of your garden
  14. Grow flowers for the bees and butterflies
